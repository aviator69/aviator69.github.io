Bhindi bhujya

  • Time: 1/2 hour
  • Serves: 4 adults
\
Ingredients Quantity
Lady finger 1 1/2 kg
Onion 3-4 small size
tomato 2-3 small size
spices as per taste

Steps:

  • Heat the oil for 5 min
  • Heat onion in oil
  • Add lady finger, tomato and spice all together
  • Let it cook for 20 min, keep mixing

Chicken karahi

  • Time: 45 min hour
  • Serves: 4 adults
\
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 1 1/2 kg
Onion 1-2 med size
tomato 2-3 small size
spices as per taste

Steps:

  • Heat the oil for 5 min
  • Heat onion in oil
  • Add Chicken, tomato and spices all together
  • Let it cook for 20-30 min, keep mixing

Macronis

  • Time: 1/2 hour
  • Serves: 4 kids
\
Ingredients Quantity
Elbow macronis 500 grams
Onion 1 small size
tomato 1 small size
spices as per taste

Steps:

  • Boil macronis until soft
  • Put boiled macronis in oil and spices with onion and tomatoes
  • Let it cook for 15 min, keep mixing
  • Serve with tomato ketchup
